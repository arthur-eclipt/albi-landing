What it is

A children's activity app for ages 3–5. Parent and child use it together. You pick an activity card, do it in real life, then mark it complete in the app. The Original Pack has 30 activities across five categories.

Cooking
Craft
Nature
Science
Movement

How it works

Three steps. That's the whole thing.

  1. Pick a card together

    Browse the deck and choose what looks fun today.

  2. Do the activity in real life

    Phone goes down. The activity happens off-screen.

  3. Mark it complete

    Tap done and watch the collection fill up over time.
